ARCHIVE ACCESS

MLKSHK is a living cultural archive — built deliberately and released selectively.
Access is not public. It is granted.

What the MLKSHK Archive Is

MLKSHK ARCHIVE operates as a curated archive of sound, capturing the atmosphere, energy, and identity of iconic musical movements and locations.

Our catalogue is continuously expanding and fully owned in-house. Every release is built as part of a wider universe — not for trends, not for volume, and not for short-term gain.

We don’t chase streams or clout.
We preserve worlds.

How Access Works

Licensing through MLKSHK is staged and selective.

New partners are typically granted access to a limited number of tracks. This allows both sides to understand alignment, usage, and creative intent before expanding access.

Extended access, archive-level licensing, and long-term partnerships are offered gradually and reviewed over time. Access may evolve — and may also be revoked.

Availability is limited by design.

Licensing Philosophy

MLKSHK licences deliberately and in limited capacity.

Not all archives are available to all partners. Certain collections are capped, retired, or reserved for specific use cases. Rights are licensed, never transferred.

We prioritise projects that respect the music, the culture it represents, and the long-term value of the archive.

If your project aligns, we’ll talk.

All submissions are reviewed individually.
