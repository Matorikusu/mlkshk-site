What This Is

MLKSHK is an independent music studio and label building long-form sound archives inspired by cultural landmarks, movements, and moments in time. We don’t produce stock music, and access is granted selectively, per project.

What Access Means

Approved partners may request permission to use music for campaigns, content, fashion, events, and other cultural projects. All music is written, produced, and cleared in-house.

The Archive

The MLKSHK archive is a growing collection of sound worlds — each inspired by a specific place, era, or movement. Current collections include The Warehouse, Shoom, Rum Runner, Blitz. New collections are added monthly, new music weekly. Access is about alignment, not volume.
