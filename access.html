ARCHIVE ACCESS

MLKSHK is a privately curated music archive built around culture, location, and era. Access is granted selectively to projects that align with the archive.

What You’re Accessing

MLKSHK is not a stock library. Each archive is a complete body of work — created in-house to capture the sound and atmosphere of specific cultural moments. New archives are added continuously.

How Access Works

Approved applicants typically begin with access to individual tracks or selected archives. Expanded access is offered gradually, based on project alignment and usage.

Selective Licensing

Not all archives are available to all partners. Licences are project-specific, time-bound, and reviewed individually. Some collections may be limited or retired as the archive evolves.

Access Is Earned

Access may be expanded, limited, or withdrawn depending on how the archive is used. We prioritise partners who respect the integrity of the work and use it with intent.
