ARCHIVE ACCESS

MLKSHK is a living cultural archive — built deliberately, released selectively. Access is not public. It is granted.

What MLKSHK Is

MLKSHK Digital operates as a curated archive of sound, capturing the atmosphere, energy, and identity of iconic musical movements and locations.

Our catalogue is continuously expanding and fully owned in-house. We do not chase trends, streams, or volume distribution — we preserve worlds.

How Access Works

Licensing through MLKSHK is staged.

New partners are typically granted access to one or two selected tracks or collections. This allows us to understand how our work is used and ensures alignment on both sides.

Expanded access, archive-level licensing, and long-term partnerships are offered selectively and reviewed over time. Access may evolve — or be withdrawn.

Licensing Philosophy

We license deliberately and in limited capacity.

Not all archives are available to all partners. Certain collections are capped, retired, or reserved for special use. Rights are licensed, never transferred.

If your project aligns with our catalogue and values, we’re open to a conversation. If it doesn’t, we won’t force it.

Request Consideration

Submissions are reviewed individually. Access is granted selectively and may be limited, expanded, or revoked at our discretion.
