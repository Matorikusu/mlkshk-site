ARCHIVE ACCESS

MLKSHK is a privately curated archive of cultural sound — built to preserve atmosphere, not chase trends. Access is granted selectively to projects that align with our catalogue and ethos.

A Living Cultural Archive

MLKSHK Digital is not a traditional music library. It is an ever-expanding archive of original recordings, designed to capture the sound, energy, and attitude of specific eras, locations, and subcultures. Each archive is produced in-house and curated as a complete body of work.

How Access Works

Access to MLKSHK is granted in stages. Approved applicants typically begin with the ability to licence individual tracks or selected archives. Expanded access, including full catalogue or partnership-level licensing, is offered selectively over time.

Selective Licensing

We licence deliberately. Not all archives are available to all partners, and availability may change as our catalogue grows. Usage is reviewed on a project-by-project basis to ensure alignment and long-term value for both parties.

Access Is Granted, Not Guaranteed

All licences are time-bound and usage-specific. Access may be expanded, limited, or revoked based on how the music is used. We prioritise collaborators who treat the archive with respect and creative intent.

Request Access
