ARCHIVE ACCESS

MLKSHK is a living archive of cultural sound — built deliberately, released selectively, and preserved in full. Access is granted, not assumed.

A Curated Digital Archive

MLKSHK Digital operates as a private archive rather than a public catalogue. Each collection is designed around a specific place, era, or cultural moment — interpreted through modern production and released as part of a wider universe.

We do not chase trends or volume. We build bodies of work intended to live long beyond their first use.

How Access Works

Access to MLKSHK is granted in stages. Approved applicants typically begin with limited use — individual tracks or selected archives — allowing us to understand how our work is represented.

As relationships develop, access may expand. In some cases, access may also be reduced or withdrawn.

This approach allows us to protect the integrity of the archive while building long-term creative partnerships.

Licensing Philosophy

MLKSHK licenses selectively. Not all archives are available to all partners, and full-catalogue access is limited by design.

We prioritise alignment, restraint, and quality of use over scale. Our catalogue remains fully owned and controlled by MLKSHK at all times.

If your project fits, we’ll explore what level of access makes sense.
