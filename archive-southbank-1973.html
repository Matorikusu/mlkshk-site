MLKSHK Archive: Southbank Undercroft 1973

Archive Revival

MLKSHK Archive: Southbank Undercroft 1973 captures the iconic London skatepark, a hub for skate culture, street music, and urban creativity. Explore the sights, sounds, and rhythms of the Undercroft.

History & Legacy

Established in 1973, the Southbank Undercroft became a mecca for skateboarders, street performers, and underground music fans. Its raw concrete environment fostered a tight-knit community and inspired countless creatives, influencing street culture across the UK.

Today, MLKSHK Archive preserves this energy through curated digital audio time capsules, celebrating the sonic and cultural heritage of this legendary skate spot.
